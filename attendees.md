## Save the Date!

Orlando Code Camp 2026 will be held at Seminole State College in Sanford, FL on **April 11, 2026** from **7:30 AM to 5:00 PM**. We're looking forward to seeing you! Here's some helpful information about the event:

## Food

The following will be provided to attendees:

- **Breakfast** and coffee will be provided by Dunkin
- **Lunch** will be provided by Jason's Deli - it will include sandwiches, chips, and various drinks (place your lunch order when you register!)
- An **afternoon snack**
- **Coffee, soda, and water** (all day!)

There will additionally be an **after-party** where food will be available!

## Raffle

Thanks to our excellent **[sponsors](/sponsors/)**, we'll be having a raffle for some fun prizes!

In order to participate:

1. Visit each of our sponsors for a stamp on your sponsors bingo card (provided on arrival at the event)
2. Drop off your bingo card at the ONETUG desk in the lobby (rotunda) at some point during the afternoon (make sure that your **name is written on the card**!) - you'll then receive a raffle ticket to hold onto until the raffle event
3. You **must be present** for the raffle give-away at the end of the event in order to win!
4. Only one (1) raffle ticket per attendee
